# Strategy Narrative

Prose forces the clear thinking that bullets let you skip. Write a tight narrative (one to six pages).

## Structure
- **Context** - the situation and why it matters now.
- **Problem / opportunity** - sharply framed, with evidence.
- **The insight** - the non-obvious thing you believe that others do not.
- **The strategy** - where you will play and how you will win; and what you are NOT doing.
- **The plan** - the few moves, sequenced, with rough owners and timing.
- **Risks / what must be true** - the load-bearing assumptions, honestly.
- **The ask** - the decision or resources you need.

## Rules
- Lead with the thesis; do not bury it on page four.
- Specific and falsifiable beats sweeping and safe.
- Cut every sentence that does not change a decision.

## Output
Offer to save as a Markdown file openable in Google Docs.
