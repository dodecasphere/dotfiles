# Backlog

A list of one-off items or ideas that can either be done quickly or are the
seed of an idea that deserves a full spec/PRD first. Single flat list,
roughly highest to lowest priority. This is the one place unresolved work
lives - a deferred requirement, an accepted-not-fixed finding, a descoped
bug, or a feature idea that isn't ready to build all go here, not scattered
across a PRD's Open Questions, a findings doc, or somewhere else that
nobody re-reads.

**Working the list:** pick from the top, take a closer look, decide whether
it's actually worth doing, then either do it or remove it. Anything done or
rejected comes off this list immediately - it doesn't linger. Re-prioritize
freely as items get resolved or new ones surface.
