# Metrics Tree

## Produce
- **North Star** - the one metric that best captures delivered customer value (not revenue, not a vanity count). State why it is the right one.
- **Input metrics** - the three to five levers that drive the North Star; these are what teams actually move.
- **Leading vs lagging** - mark which indicators predict versus confirm.
- **Guardrail metrics** - what must NOT degrade (latency, churn, support load, unit cost).
- **Tracking plan** - the events and properties to instrument for each metric, and where they fire.

## Rules
- Reject vanity metrics (page views, total registered users, raw downloads) unless directly tied to value.
- Every metric needs a definition, a current baseline, and a target.
- A good metric is hard to game without delivering real value. Flag any metric that is easy to game.
